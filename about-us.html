Sickle Cell-Thalessemia-Hemophilia Mission

Unique Modality of Health Care & Prevention

Reaching people and touching lives




The driving force for the initiation of STHM are the person suffering with Sickle cell, thalassemia and Hemophilia. In every corner of the state (be it any district, village, urban or rural), the ignorant attitude towards these diseases mismanagement, poor diagnostic services, led the foundation of STHM. It is founded as a non-profit, non-governmental organization, registered in the year 2018, Reg. no. 41131808632; Dt. 19-11-18.

It got started with commitment to increase the awareness about the disease and its treatment. The mission works as a bridge and narrows the gap within the physicians, patients, diagnostic services, and facilitates the healthcare management starting from self care to health services (Govt. or Private). All the three diseases are preventable and cure is beyond the scope of an individual but we are still lagging in our efforts of prevention. So, the mission decides to take strong initiative.

a) For care and management of the existing patient

b) To prevent further inheritance of these diseases.

Our effort is : To restore normalcy in the life of patients living with Thalassemia, sickle cell disease and Hemophilia regardless of their financial status by enabling access to improved care within limited resources. To sensitize public about the diseases and encourage voluntary blood donations. To boost the knowledge of "right time to act", answer the ignorance by providing enough information about disease, management, cure and its prevention.
