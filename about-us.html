Sickle Cell-Thalessemia-Hemophilia Mission

Unique Modality of Health Care & Prevention

Reaching people and touching lives


    STHM (Sickle cell, Thalassemia and Hemophilia Mission) is an organization intended to promote the state of art treatment to individuals with sickle cell, thalassemia and hemophilia. The mission started since 2017 and was registered as an organisation in the year of 2018. It spreads awareness and propagate various preventive measures with a goal to minimize or decrease the burden of these disorders.
