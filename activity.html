Activities:

Vision

  • STHM Activity domains: Genetic Awareness camps Motivational blood donation campaign Camp of Investigation and treatment support. Facilitates Hospital based speciality care for STH group of patients Adds direction and networking to prevention through genetic counseling and pre-natal diagnosis. Blood bank support for allo-immunized cases. Educate the patient to stand for their own needs In Govt. health sector of various districts. Laisoning with the officials for essential requirements at their respective hospitals. Extend clinical support to other thalassemia, Hemophilia societies. Levels of STHM Activity At ground level: We formulate the network with District headquarter hospitals, blood bank officers, local stakeholders, NGOs, to pursue diagnosis, treatment for existing patients and spread the message of prevention at different platforms. Through district visit, we reach every common individual to participate in the mission in any form, either as blood donor or in promotion of prevention. At Hospital level (SUM Hospital, Bhubaneswar): Deliver OPD consultation for treatment and Genetic counseling. Provide Leuco-depleted fresh packed red blood cell. Antigen negative packed red cell for alloimmunized cases. Extended red cell phenotyping. Facilitates blood transfusion at Day care as per the guidelines. Comprehensive Health care service for complicated cases. Networking and co-ordination for prenatal diagnosis within fetal medicine and genetic lab. At Mega Camps: Clinic visit in districts and villages Blood Investigation : CBC, biochemical test like ferritin, Liver function test Screening for Transfusion transmitted infection Sample collection for HLA testing Mutational analysis for prevention Screening of fit candidates for bone marrow transplantation. Counseling sessions and consultation. Blood donation drive and Organize Blood donation camp.
